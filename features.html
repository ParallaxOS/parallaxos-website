Operations

Run the business from a single dashboard.

The morning briefing, the AI command bar, the scheduler, the timesheets, and the invoicing — every operational decision in one workflow.

▦

Dashboard

Live overview of shifts today, unassigned shifts, blocked workers, expiring certifications. Triage your day in under ten seconds. Sovereign Compliance Status cards surface exactly where action is needed: TRIFR ledger, insurance, SWMS returns, near-miss queue.

⌘K

AI Command Bar

Plain-English questions, no menu hunting. "Compile my Avetta stats for February" returns a verified, sealed compliance pack in three seconds. The Auto agent routes every query to the correct specialist agent automatically.

▤

Intelligent Scheduler

Float × Monday, purpose-built for compliance-gated rail rostering. Four views: Schedule (swim lanes), Board (kanban), Jobs, People. Every worker row shows their live FAID fatigue bar. Blocked workers cannot be assigned. Open shifts flagged in red.

⌖

GPS-Verified Timesheets

Every timesheet is GPS-stamped at clock-on and clock-off, then SHA-256 cryptographically sealed at submission. Tamper-evident and ONRSR-defensible. Supervisor and worker verify on site via QR handshake. Bulk-approve in one tap.

$

EBA-Aware Invoicing

Select a job and a period — ParallaxOS pulls every approved timesheet and auto-applies night-shift penalties (125% / 150%), overtime (150% / 200%), travel and meal allowances from the Enterprise Agreement. One tap to Xero or MYOB.

!

Overdue invoice tracking

Outstanding invoices surface on the dashboard, not buried on the Invoicing page. INV-2026-001 at $34,980 overdue 11 days is visible immediately, with a one-tap payment reminder to the client.

Compliance

Caught before the shift, not at the gate.

Every worker's RIW card, FAID fatigue score, medical clearance, and certification continuously monitored. Workers are automatically prevented from shift assignment when credentials lapse — not flagged after the fact.

RIW

Compliance Management

Single view with CURRENT / EXPIRING / MISSING / BLOCKED status indicators per worker. Automatic alerts at 30, 14, and 7 days before any certification expires. "Resolve" routes directly to the booking or recertification action — three navigation steps reduced to one.

+

Medical Booking

Network-aware. Cat 1 medicals (Safeworking Officers): full assessment + ECG + blood test 48 hrs prior. Cat 3 (Track Maintainers, Lookouts): once to age 40, then five-yearly — with the TfNSW override that requires Cat 3 every five years regardless of age. AHPs GPS-sorted by distance with live RIW Direct Upload status.

⊘

Digital D&A Testing

Paper Drug & Alcohol forms destroyed by weather is one of the most common HSEQ failures in Australian rail. ParallaxOS digitises every test: GPS-stamped, cryptographically sealed, with a built-in random selection algorithm. Full ONRSR audit trail. Zero paper.

📁

Document Hub

Every document required for corridor access — SWMS returns, Verifications of Competency, Permits to Work, ITPs, pre-starts — tracked against job folders with overdue alerts. Every uploaded document indexed by AI: ask any question about a SWMS or network rule and receive a cited, specific answer.

Field Operations

The crew on the corridor, in real time.

GPS-verified worker positions, asset compliance with VoC enforcement, and a worker portal designed for hi-vis gloves at 04:00 in a tunnel.

⚙

Asset & Operator Matrix

Every piece of fleet plant with live service status, fuel level, and engine hours. The operator compliance matrix shows exactly which workers hold the required Verification of Competency for each machine. Assets overdue for service are automatically locked from assignment until cleared.

⚲

Live GPS Supervision

Every worker on the rail corridor in real time. Tap any pin: GPS coordinates, battery level, clock-on time, pre-start status, FAID score. Hazard reports appear on the map immediately. Supervisor approvals queue visible as a badge on the dashboard.

📱

Worker Portal — mobile

My Shifts feature card: job, location, time, supervisor contact, required documents, weather. FAID fatigue bar visible before accepting a shift. Clock on with GPS stamp. Pre-start checklist works entirely offline. Emergency tab: tap-to-call 000, ONRSR, Network Control, and the nearest hospital.

SOC

Supervisor Portal — tablet

Live GPS map of the crew, blocked workers visually distinct and unselectable, one-tap timesheet approval with rejection notes. Log a near-miss or incident in under sixty seconds — tap, photo, GPS-stamp, submit. Bottom navigation, glove-safe targets.

Edge Mode

Built for tunnels, cuttings, and dead zones.

Rail corridors are not WiFi cafés. ParallaxOS is offline-first by design. When connectivity drops, the brutalist orange Edge Mode banner activates and the platform continues to function fully. Pre-starts, clock on/off, D&A tests, evidence photos, incident logs — all captured locally.

Every offline write is cryptographically sealed with SHA-256 to prevent tampering, then synced automatically when signal returns. Conflict resolution is last-write-wins with full audit trail preservation.
